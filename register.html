Why to sponsor The Influence Exchange and Awards – Dubai?

ENHANCED BRAND AWARENESS

Join the largest conference for marketers and creators in the region, The Influencer Exchange & Awards, to enhance your brand identity. Gain online exposure and have your company logo featured on the home page, as well as a dedicated sponsor page on the IE&A website (www.ie&a.com).

CONNECT WITH YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE

Take advantage of our sponsoring & branding opportunities to display your brand to over 300 attendees, including industry enthusiasts, influencers, and marketers.

ENJOY THE PUBLICITY

Get featured in pre-event PR and social media promotions in the lead-up to the event. Get a sponsorship announcement exclusively dedicated to your brand across IE&A's marketing. channels.

PROMOTE YOUR CAMPAIGNS

Utilize IE&A as a platform to gain exposure for your planned campaigns and launches, such as contests and visitor incentives that require data collection and sign-ups.

BE SEEN AS A KEY MARKET PLAYER

As the largest conference for marketers and creators in the region, IE&A offers an excellent platform for exposure and recognition of your company as a significant player in the market.

NETWORKING OPPORTUNITIES

Dubai offers an opportunity to network with the brightest minds in the creator and influencer marketing community. The event brings together top brands, creators, agencies, and technology experts who are driving the creator economy forward. As a sponsor, you'll have access to insights and debates that will equip you with several strategies and new ideas to drive incredible results in an authentic, transparent, and measurable manner.

Relationship Building

The event offers a day of networking opportunities that you can use to cement relationships with prospects. You can treat them to networking coffee sessions and the event also features more content, exhibitors, and feature zones that you can leverage to showcase your products or services.

Comprehensive Topics

The event covers various topics relevant to agencies, brands, and retailers, including creator identification, the creator economy, social experience and live shopping, creator partnerships for funnel success, harnessing the network effect, technologies for creator success, regulation and standardization, gaining decentralized business value from creators, outcome-based influencer marketing, the revised creator marketing matrix, and ambassadorial commerce of the future.

Concerns Deciphered and Solved

The event offers a platform for brands to have their common concerns and challenges deciphered and solved. As a sponsor, you'll have the opportunity to share your concerns and get an action plan to follow with your next campaign to ensure you meet your agreed KPIs or client objectives.

Connections that Count

Sponsoring the event gives you an opportunity to connect with leading agencies, technologies, and influencer marketing consultants.

Brand Exposure

Sponsoring the event puts your brand in front of industry-leading retailers, increases your brand recognition, and makes you a talking point with international partners. You can also demonstrate your thought leadership and expertise in influencer marketing, a rapidly growing and evolving field. In summary, sponsoring the Influence Exchange and Awards – Dubai offers an excellent opportunity to network, build relationships, gain insights, showcase your products or services, solve common concerns, and connect with leading agencies and technologies in the influencer marketing industry.
