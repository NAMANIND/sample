ABOUT

The objective of the conference is to bring together influencers and marketing professionals to exchange knowledge, explore industry trends, and foster networking opportunities. The conference aims to provide valuable insights and practical strategies to help attendees enhance their marketing efforts through influencer collaborations.

Middle East influencer marketing has the second fastest growth rate in the world with the sector growing from around $300 million at present to an expected reach of $1.3 billion in a few years. The scope of marketing and brand awareness is changing at a fast pace and where the traditional method of promotions and branding stands ineffective, marketers are on a look-out for ways to reach out to consumers through influencers without direct advertising. In an industry full of potential, join us as we unite brands, influencers, agencies, and technology to empower the community and reimagine the future of Influencer marketing.

INFLUENCER MARKETING IN THE UAE

As per a recent study by YouGov, 94 per cent of in-house marketers in the UAE believe that social media influencer marketing plays a major role in the success of their brands. Every three in four UAE residents follow some social media influencers, out of which 29% follow health and fitness influencers. These numbers reflect the interest of brands to spend a significant part of their advertising budgets with influencer partnerships, especially in industries like beauty, fashion, food, lifestyle, tech, and entertainment. The UAE's National Media Council’s (NMC) initiative of introduction of three tiered influencer legislation, to resolve and regulate the social media marketplace in order to protect consumers and brands is a move that will not only assist with market regulation but will also allow for creative partnerships between brands and influencers to flourish. As per the emarketer data, 74.5 per cent of marketers are using influencer marketing today, and this will hit 88.7 per cent in 2026. In the Middle East, growth rate of Influence marketing is between 30-40 per cent a year. Influencer marketing in the Middle East has grown to $16.4 billion in 2022
